Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Overview

Song Chords is a personal web application for displaying and formatting song chord sheets. This policy describes how your data is handled.

Data collected

Song Chords does not collect any personal data on a server. All data is stored locally in your browser (localStorage):

  • Your songs and their display settings
  • Your interface preferences (font size, colors, columns)
  • Your Ultimate Guitar credentials (if you use the web search feature)

Google Drive

If you choose to connect Google Drive, the application accesses your Google account solely to sync your songs in a dedicated folder. The application:

  • Only reads files it has created itself in the Song Chords folder
  • Does not share your data with any third party
  • Does not store your Google token on a server (it stays in your browser)

Ultimate Guitar

If you use the web search feature, your Ultimate Guitar credentials are:

  • Stored only in your browser's localStorage
  • Transmitted encrypted (HTTPS) to our server proxy for authentication with the Ultimate Guitar API
  • Never stored or logged on the server side

Cookies and tracking

Song Chords does not use any cookies, analytics tools, or trackers.

Data deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage (Browser settings → Site data).

Contact

For any questions, you can open an issue on the project repository.
